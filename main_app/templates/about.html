- Welcome to Dino Park!

A unique way to stay on task and remain focused.

Turn your focus sessions into cute hatchable dinosaurs.

Dino Park gamifies the popular Pomodoro technique often used for productive moments in life. Watch as your Dino Park grows, and see a manifestation of your productivity through the Parks you have built over each passing day.
